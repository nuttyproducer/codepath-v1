Exercise 1 — Fix the colour contrast failures

WCAG 2.1 AA requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold). Find and fix all contrast failures below.

Use the WebAIM Contrast Checker or browser DevTools (Accessibility tab → Contrast) to measure.

FAIL

Gray text on white background — ratio: ~2.5:1 ❌ (WCAG AA requires 4.5:1 for body text)

FAIL

Yellow text on white — ratio: ~1.07:1 ❌ (Almost completely invisible)

FAIL
Light text on light blue — ratio: ~2.1:1 ❌ (Need a much darker blue or white text)
PASS ✓

Dark text on white — ratio: ~16:1 ✅ (This is what you should aim for)

PASS ✓
White text on dark blue — ratio: ~8.6:1 ✅ (Exceeds AA and AAA requirements)

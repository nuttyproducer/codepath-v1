What is semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML means using HTML elements for their intended purpose ‚Äî not just for how they look. A <button> communicates "click me" to screen readers. A <nav> communicates "this is navigation". A <main> communicates "this is the main content of the page".

Why it matters

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect."

‚Äî Tim Berners-Lee, inventor of the World Wide Web

Semantic HTML is the foundation of accessibility. Screen readers, search engines, and browser extensions all rely on it.

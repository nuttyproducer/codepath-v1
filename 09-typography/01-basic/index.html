Exercise 3 — Readable body text

Set max-width: 65ch on body copy and compare reading comfort.

The importance of line length

Research in typography shows that the optimal line length for comfortable reading is between 45 and 75 characters per line. Lines that are too long cause the reader's eye to lose track when jumping to the next line. Lines that are too short cause the eye to jump back too frequently, disrupting reading rhythm.

The ch unit in CSS represents the width of the "0" character in the current font. Setting max-width: 65ch is a simple, font-relative way to ensure readable line lengths.
